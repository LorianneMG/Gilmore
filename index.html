About Me

UX/UI & accesibilty have become very important to me and I'm interersted in making sure all sites are available for all people.

Were you one of the people who taught yourself a little HTML back in the Myspace days? Me too!! I did it so I could make some gorgeous looking pages! Well here I am, almost 20 years later and still doing the same thing, I just got way better at it.

My dream is to make custom projects that make the internet less boring. There's a lot of "cookie-cutter" websites out there that are boring and I've always been one who wants to stand out from the crowd. I love customization and personalization and I want to bring that to my clients.

My myriad of skills range from the simplicity of HTML and CSS to the more complex coding languages of Javascript and Python with a plethora of knowledge in between. I am deeply passionate about the digital media arts. I have experience with Photoshop and Illustrator as well as other graphic design programs used today.
